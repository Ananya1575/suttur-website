Medical Institutes

JSS Ayurveda Medical College

JSSMVP established an Ayurveda Medical College in Mysuru in 1996 to promote holistic healing, medical education, and research. Recognized by CCIM and RGUHS, it offers modern infrastructure for studying this ancient Indian system of medicine.

An Ayurveda Medical College in Mysuru was established by JSSMVP in 1996, to deal with human ailments in terms of clinical care, medical education and research. Ayurveda is one of the world’s oldest holistic healing system with historical roots in India. Besides, this ancient diagnostic and treatment method is gaining popularity in recent times. The institution is well-equipped with best in class infrastructure. Both Central Council for Indian Medicine and Rajiv Gandhi University of Health Sciences recognizes the courses offered by the College.

JSS Institute of Nature Cure and Yogic Sciences College

JSSINYS, founded in 1997, is a leading institution for Naturopathy and Yogic Sciences. It offers a 5.5-year BNYS program, combining academic excellence with practical training.

JSSINYS, established in 1997, is a premier institution dedicated to the scientific study and practice of Naturopathy and Yogic Sciences. Located on a scenic campus, it offers a 5.5-year BNYS program (4.5 years of academics + 1 year of internship) affiliated with Tamil Nadu Dr. MGR Medical University. JSSINYS focuses on providing students with a strong foundation in traditional healing methods while emphasizing research and clinical excellence. The institution boasts a well-equipped campus with modern facilities for learning and patient care. JSSINYS is recognized by leading bodies like CCRYN and NIN, ensuring quality education and training. The curriculum combines theoretical knowledge with hands-on experience through lectures, seminars, and workshops. With a focus on holistic healing, JSSINYS aims to produce skilled and compassionate graduates who can contribute significantly to the field of Naturopathy and Yoga.

JSS college of Physiotherapy

JSS College of Physiotherapy, established in 1999, is a leading institution dedicated to training skilled physiotherapists. It offers undergraduate and postgraduate programs, preparing students for successful careers in the healthcare field.

Recognized by Rajiv Gandhi University of Medical Sciences, Bengaluru, JSS College of Physiotherapy aims to provide students with a comprehensive understanding of human movement and its implications for health and well-being. The curriculum emphasizes both theoretical knowledge and practical skills through hands-on experience at JSS Hospital and collaborations with various healthcare organizations. The college boasts well-equipped laboratories, a comprehensive library, and a focus on student development through sports and recreational activities.

JSS Institute of Speech and Hearing, Mysuru

JSS Institute of Speech and Hearing, established in Mysuru in 2001-02, trains professionals in speech and hearing rehabilitation. Affiliated with the University of Mysore, it offers B.Sc., MASLP, MSc (Audiology), and MSc (Speech-Language Pathology) courses recognized by the Rehabilitation Council of India.

JSS Institute of Speech and Hearing was established in Mysore in 2001-02 to meet the increasing need for qualified professionals in speech and hearing. It is affiliated to the University of Mysore and is recognized by the Rehabilitation Council of India, Govt. of India. The institute offers a four-year B.Sc. degree in Speech and Hearing Postgraduate courses in MASLP, MSc(Audiology) and MSc(Speech-Language Pathology). Mysuru University recognizes the Institute as a Research Centre. These courses are professional courses designed to produce speech and hearing specialists. They are broadly classified as Speech Pathologists and Audiologists. The Institute trains the speech and hearing professionals to cater to the health issues related to habilitation and rehabilitation of individuals of all age groups (from infants to the very old) with speech and hearing problems. The staff and students of the institute conduct and attend speech and hearing camps organised by Rotary and Lion clubs in various places. These camps help in bringing awareness among the rural population about speech and hearing problems and remedies available.

JSS Institute of Speech and Hearing, Dharwad

JJSSMVP established the JSS Institute of Speech and Hearing in Dharwad in 2014, offering BASLP courses and quality clinical services for communication disorders. Affiliated with Karnataka University and recognized by RCI, it also serves as a rehabilitation center under Karnataka's Cochlear Implant Scheme.

JSSMVP established its second Speech & Hearing institute in Dharwad in 2014. JSS Institute of Speech and Hearing, Dharwad is located in a lush green campus measuring around 80 acres, near Kelageri- lake, Dharwad. It offers Bachelor of Audiology and Speech-Language Pathology (BASLP) course to provide an opportunity for aspiring students to pursue graduation in the field of speech and hearing. The institute has state-of-the-art facilities and conducts clinical activities in the assessment and treatment of various communication disorders. This institute is affiliated to Karnataka University, Dharwad. In addition to this, Rehabilitation Council of India, Govt. of India recognizes it. The institute is also recognised by the Suvarna Arogya Suraksha Trust (SAST) as a rehabilitation entre of the Govt. of Karnataka’s Cochlear Implant Scheme. The objective of the institute is to provide quality education and affordable clinical services to the public education. The institute also carries out need-based research programmmes.

DJSS College of Nursing, Mysuru

Established in 1997, JSS College of Nursing, Mysuru, is the city's first nursing college and the pioneer in offering postgraduate nursing education. With a mission to uphold quality standards, it is guided by experienced faculty dedicated to excellence in nursing education.

The JSS College of Nursing was started in November 1997 by the foresight and vision of His Holiness Jagadguru Sri Shivarathreeshwara Deshikendra Mahaswamiji to prepare professional Nurses at the university level in order to improve standards of nursing service through quality nursing education. The idea was conceived in 1990 and nurtured for seven long years. This is the first college of nursing started in the city of Mysore as well as the first college of nursing in Mysore to start a post-graduation course in nursing. Its motto is to maintain the standards of nursing education and all efforts are made to deploy well qualified and experienced faculty to achieve the goal.

JSS College of Pharmacy, Mysore

Jagadguru Sri Dr Shivarathri Rajendra Mahaswamjigalavaru, the 23rd pontiff of Sri Suttur Veerasimhasana Math was the architect and founder president of JSS Mahavidyapeetha, which came into being in 1954. With the divine inspiration of Sri Swamiji, the JSS College of Pharmacy was started in the year 1973 in Mysuru.

JSS College of Pharmacy is a constituent college of JSS University, Mysore. JSS University is recognized by Ministry of Human Resource Development, Government of India on 28th May 2008 and has declared Jagadguru Sri Shivarathreeshwara University (JSSU), Mysore, Karnataka, as deemed. The college offers a comprehensive package of pharmacy education and training opportunities beginning with the Diploma in Pharmacy (D.Pharm), B.Pharm (Practice), Bachelor of Pharmacy (B.Pharm), Doctor of Pharmacy (Pharm.D.), Master of Pharmacy (M.Pharm) and Doctoral (PhD) programs, Residency Programme in Oncology & Nephrology. Postgraduate diploma and certificate courses are also offered to the students as an add-on to the regular programs. The college houses some of the nation`s recognized researchers and educates some of the brightest students nationally and internationally. The college has a Drug Testing Laboratory which is approved by Govt. of Karnataka and is NABL accreditated. The institute is a renowned leader in pharmaceutical education, research and practice. This benchmarking has been recognized nationally in the form of accreditation by National Board of Accreditation (NBA), India. As a part of global recognition, the Pharm.D. Program has been certified by Accreditation Council for Pharmacy Education (ACPE), USA. It is presently ranked as top 10 pharmacy college in the country (by NIRF, MHRD, Govt. of India). The college has active Training and Placement Cell which conducts campus drive annually. A large pool of Pharmaceutical Companies, Research Organisations participate in this drive.

College of Pharmacy, Ooty

JSS College of Pharmacy, Ooty, established in 1980, is a premier institution offering D.Pharm., B.Pharm., M.Pharm. (10 specializations), Pharm.D., and PhD programs. Accredited by NAAC with an A+ grade, it is internationally recognized, including ACPE certification for its Pharm.D. program, the first in the Asia Pacific region.

Established in 1980 with D.Pharm. program, JSS College of Pharmacy, Ooty is a constituent college of Jagadguru Sri Shivarathreeswara University (JSS University), Mysuru since 2008, and positioned today as one of the premier postgraduate and research institution imparting D.Pharm., B.Pharm., M.Pharm., (10 Specializations) Pharm.D., and PhD. Programs. The ‘Add-On’ PG Diploma and Certificate courses offered to the students and scholars are for value addition and nurturing their knowledge in inter-disciplinary subjects. The College and the JSS AHER are accredited by the National Assessment and Accreditation Council (NAAC) with `A+` Grade. Our B.Pharm. Program is accredited by NBA, New Delhi and the Pharm.D. Program is awarded with international certification by ACPE, USA for the first time in the Asia Pacific Region. The Drug Testing Laboratory is accredited by NABL. Both the college and CADRAT are certified with ISO 9001:2015for the quality management systems. The college is ranked 8th in India by NIRF ranking 2019. As strong believers in ‘Team Play’, our focus is on collaboration instead of competition resulting in National and International MoUs to foster teaching-learning, research through faculty and student exchange, consultancy, training programs, etc., JSS College of Pharmacy, Ooty has an esteemed legacy in advancing pharmaceutical education, practice and research and shaping pharmacy professionals on par with international standards and requirements.
