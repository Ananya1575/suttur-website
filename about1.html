Suttur Mutt: A Beacon of Spiritual Enlightenment

Over a Millennium of Wisdom, Service, and Spiritual Heritage

JSS Suttur Mutt: A Thousand-Year Legacy

Suttur Math, a pilgrim centre with over a thousand years of rich history, stands as a testament to spiritual enlightenment and societal progress. The Pontiffs of the Math have selflessly dedicated themselves to the spiritual and educational development of people from all walks of life, transcending religious and cultural boundaries.

As a seat of knowledge and wisdom, the Math has tirelessly worked for human welfare since its inception. Its influence has grown far beyond the banks of the River Kapila in Karnataka, touching lives across India and the world.

Spiritual Mission: Bridging Ancient Wisdom with Modern World

The JSS Spiritual Mission, working in harmony with like-minded organizations, has embarked on a noble journey to spread India's rich socio-cultural and spiritual heritage across the globe. Through religious discourses, yoga practices, and the dissemination of spiritual knowledge via books, cassettes, and CDs, the Mission makes ancient wisdom accessible to all.

Established under 501c3 with a church status, the Mission's aim is to serve society by helping citizens imbibe lasting values rooted in tradition. These universal principles transcend the boundaries of caste, creed, community, and race, offering a path to a safe and peaceful life for all who seek it.
