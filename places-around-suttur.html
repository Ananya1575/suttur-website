Explore Around Suttur

25 km

Mysore Palace

The magnificent Mysore Palace, also known as Amba Vilas Palace, is one of India's most famous tourist attractions. This architectural marvel combines Indo-Saracenic, Dravidian, and British styles.

What's Special

  • • Light and Sound show
  • • Royal artifacts collection
  • • Grand architecture
Get Directions
8 km

Nanjangud Temple

Known as 'Dakshina Kashi', this ancient temple dedicated to Lord Shiva is renowned for its magnificent architecture and spiritual significance. The temple complex is a masterpiece of Dravidian architecture.

What's Special

  • • Ancient sculptures
  • • Traditional rituals
  • • Sacred water tank
Get Directions
30 km

Chamundi Hills

A sacred hill hosting the famous Chamundeshwari Temple, offering panoramic views of Mysore city. The hill features a historic stone stairway of 1000 steps and several significant monuments.

What's Special

  • • Panoramic city views
  • • Ancient temple architecture
  • • Nandi statue
Get Directions
45 km

BR Hills Wildlife Sanctuary

Biligiriranga Hills offers a unique blend of wildlife and spirituality. This bio-diverse sanctuary is home to various species of flora and fauna, alongside the ancient Biligiriranga Temple.

What's Special

  • • Wildlife safaris
  • • Nature trails
  • • Ancient temple
Get Directions
60 km

Bylakuppe Monastery

The second largest Tibetan settlement outside Tibet, featuring magnificent monasteries, colorful prayer flags, and authentic Tibetan culture. The Golden Temple is a must-visit attraction.

What's Special

  • • Golden Temple
  • • Tibetan culture
  • • Authentic cuisine
Get Directions
90 km

Coorg (Kodagu)

Known as the 'Scotland of India', Coorg offers misty hills, coffee plantations, and waterfalls. This hill station is perfect for nature lovers and adventure enthusiasts.

What's Special

  • • Coffee plantations
  • • Abbey Falls
  • • Raja's Seat
