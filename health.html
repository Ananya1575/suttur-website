Medical Institutes

JSS Hospital Mysore

JSS Hospital on MG Road, Mysuru, has a rich history dating back to 1963 when His Holiness Dr. Sri Shivarathri Rajendra Mahaswamiji established the JSS Health Centre under the JSS Medical Service Trust to provide affordable healthcare to the poor. Over time, it has grown into an 1800-bed teaching hospital with 35 specialties and super specialties, serving JSS Medical College. A pioneer in comprehensive healthcare, JSS Hospital caters to patients from rural areas across Mysuru, Chamarajanagar, Mandya, Coorg, Hassan in Karnataka, and the Nilgiris in Tamil Nadu. It serves 800–1000 outpatients daily and organizes rural health awareness programs.

JSS Ayurveda Hospital

The JSS Ayurveda Hospital began operations on MG Road, Mysuru, in August 1992, offering treatments across all Ayurvedic specialties with a focus on providing quality care to the public, particularly the rural poor. Over the past 18 years, the hospital, in association with the JSS Ayurveda Medical College, has benefited over 6 lakh patients from Mysuru, Chamarajanagar, Coorg, and Mandya. As a teaching hospital, it combines academic rigor with patient care, drawing its medical staff primarily from the prestigious JSS Ayurveda Medical College. This academic foundation ensures continuous upgrading of treatments and services. To address the rising demand for Ayurvedic healthcare, JSS Mahavidyapeetha expanded the facility, creating a 1,10,000 sq. ft. state-of-the-art hospital at Lalithadripura, Mysuru

JSS Hospital, Chamarajanagar

JSS Hospital, Chamarajanagar, is a notable addition to the extensive healthcare services offered under the patronage of Sri Suttur Math. This 100-bed facility is dedicated to providing advanced and personalized medical care to the residents of Chamarajanagar and nearby regions. Equipped with modern infrastructure, the hospital caters to a variety of healthcare needs while maintaining a focus on accessibility and affordability for the community. In addition to its core services, the hospital reflects the broader vision of JSS Mahavidyapeetha (JSSMVP), which integrates healthcare and education. Institutions under JSSMVP, such as SJCE, JSS International Schools in Ooty and Dubai, and JSS Academy of Technical Education in Noida, also include healthcare facilities that benefit students, staff, and local communities. JSS Hospital, Chamarajanagar, embodies this commitment to improving lives through compassionate care and progressive services

JSS Yoga and Nature Cure Hospital

Located in Pichanur on the Palakkad Highway in Coimbatore, this 150-bed hospital is dedicated to providing care through a drugless system of medicine. Spanning a vast 55-acre expanse of lush green surroundings, the hospital offers a serene and therapeutic environment, ideal for holistic healing and recovery. It is well-equipped with modern facilities and employs a comprehensive approach to treating a variety of ailments, emphasizing natural and non-invasive methods. The hospital is staffed by a team of highly experienced and qualified naturopaths, along with skilled and trained therapists, who specialize in managing chronic illnesses, allergies, and complex medical conditions. These professionals are committed to patient care and are available around the clock to ensure continuous and effective treatment. By combining expertise with a holistic philosophy, the hospital provides a unique and natural pathway to health and wellness for patients.
