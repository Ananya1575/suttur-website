Why Choose Suttur?

Spiritual Significance

Home to the renowned Suttur Mutt, a centuries-old spiritual and educational center. Experience the rich traditions and teachings of Basavanna in this hub of the Lingayat community.

Educational Excellence

JSS Foundation operates numerous educational institutions, providing quality education to thousands of students, with a special focus on rural education and development.

Cultural Heritage

Experience the grand Suttur Jathre, an annual fair celebrating Karnataka's rich cultural heritage through various programs and activities.

Community Service

Witness and participate in various social welfare activities, including free education, healthcare, and food distribution programs.

Scenic Beauty

Located amidst lush greenery and serene landscapes, Suttur offers a peaceful retreat perfect for meditation and spiritual reflection.

Accessibility

Conveniently located near Mysuru, making it an ideal destination to combine with visits to other iconic attractions in the region.
