JSS Mahavidyapeetha

Empowering Lives Through Education Since 1954

In Pursuit of Excellence

JSS Mahavidyapeetha (JSSMVP), a formidable educational movement, traces its origins to a humble beginning in 1928 with the establishment of a small hostel for students in Mysore. Under the visionary leadership of His Holiness Jagadguru Dr. Sri Shivarathri Rajendra Mahaswamiji, JSSMVP was formally established in 1954 and registered under the Societies Registration Act 1960.

Today, JSSMVP stands as a beacon of educational excellence, managing over 300 institutions worldwide and serving more than 100,000 students. From kindergartens to postgraduate centers and research facilities, our institutions span across India and have established presence in the United States, Mauritius, and Dubai.

The First Institute: A Humble Beginning

The journey of Suttur Mutt's educational mission began with a modest free hostel in 1928, established to support students pursuing higher education in Mysore. This pioneering initiative laid the foundation for what would become one of India's most significant educational movements. The hostel represented more than just accommodation; it embodied the Math's commitment to making education accessible to all, regardless of their social or economic background.
