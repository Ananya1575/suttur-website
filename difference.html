The Mutt provides scholarships to meritorious students residing in its hostels, facilitating access to quality education.

Flood Relief Contributions

In August 2019, the Mysuru Citizens Forum (MCF), associated with Suttur Mutt, donated ₹1 crore to the Chief Minister's Flood Relief Fund.

Source: Star of Mysore

Ayodhya Ram Temple Donation

Suttur Mutt donated ₹10 lakh towards the construction of the Sri Ram Temple in Ayodhya.

Source: Star of Mysore
